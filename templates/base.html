About Us
About FlatMatch

FlatMatch was developed and designed by Abdul Wadood. It was created to address the challenges faced by college students in finding roommates and rooms nearby their college.

Our platform aims to make it easier for students to find accommodation, especially by saving on brokerage fees when the flat is posted by its owner. We strive to make the process hassle-free for students coming from different cities.
